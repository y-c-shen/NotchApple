Hover the notch

One apple, three effects

Switch modes right in the tray. The apple remembers your pick.

✦
// default

Potion Particles

Eat the apple and Minecraft potion swirls rise across your whole screen, with a golden flash. Zero productivity value. Maximum joy.

⏳
// focus

Pomodoro

Eat to start a focus timer. A potion-effect badge counts down in the notch. When it ends: the achievement chime and an "Advancement Made!" toast.

⛔
// discipline

Distraction Blocker

Cold turkey for social media. Open a blocked site and you get Minecraft's "Connection Lost" screen instead. Eat again to reconnect.

How it works

1

Hover the notch. The tray slides down and the apple appears.

2

Choose the effect. Pick Default, Pomodoro, or Blocker right in the tray.

3

Press and hold. A ring fills as you eat — then your chosen effect fires.
